About this page

The Platform Engineering Toolbox is a practical resource designed to help teams navigate the fast-evolving field of platform engineering. It brings together useful practices, frameworks, and references to support the creation of effective developer platforms.

Our aim is to make it easier to find a starting point and choose from proven approaches. While applying platform engineering principles is always context-dependent, this toolbox offers guidance to help practitioners adapt ideas to their own environment.

All resources are grounded in real-world experience and inspired by modern approaches to scaling agility, such as those described in Sooner Safer Happier.
