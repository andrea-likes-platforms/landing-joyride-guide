PlatformOps – Platform Services Landscape Canvas

Understanding what your platforms really offer—and how they’re perceived—is the first step toward improving them. The Platform Services Landscape Canvas helps teams map and discuss the services provided by internal platforms (or platform-like functions) in a simple, visual way. It’s designed to spark shared understanding across roles, reveal gaps or overlaps, and provide a common base for planning and improvement discussions. Ideal for quick, collaborative workshops (30–60 minutes) that bring platform, product, and operations perspectives together.
